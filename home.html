Who We Are

When we were applying to study medicine, often we'd ask family friends or people in the year above for advice and tips. Not everyone has these connections, and we believe this kind of advice should be available to anyone, for free.


We are trying to bridge the gap between prospective students, and professionals who are keen to share their knowledge. On our website you can find all sorts of information, and get to know people who can support your goals.
